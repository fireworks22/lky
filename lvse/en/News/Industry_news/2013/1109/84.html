The major textile manufacturing enterprises concentrated in Western Europe and Japan, one of the main suppliers of Western Europe are BASF, BAYER, CI-BA fine chemical company. For more than a pin textile auxiliaries from the entire point of view,the annual consumption is about 2300000 tons, an increase of 25% over the early nineteen ninties, average annual growth of 2.4%.
